Meet Our Coaches

Real experience. Real passion. Real results.

Our coaching staff brings real experience, passion, and leadership to every training session. From professional fighters to experienced trainers and mentors, our team is dedicated to helping every member reach their highest potential.

At SuperBad Boxing Gym, coaching goes beyond teaching punches and footwork. We focus on discipline, accountability, mental toughness, and personal growth to help develop complete athletes and strong individuals.

What Our Coaches Provide

  • Technical boxing instruction
  • Strength & conditioning guidance
  • Amateur & professional fight preparation
  • Personal mentorship
  • Youth development
  • Performance coaching
  • Mental toughness training
