Boxing Training Program

Learn Real Boxing

Our Boxing Training Program teaches authentic boxing fundamentals including footwork, combinations, defense, conditioning, movement, ring awareness, and mental discipline. Designed for beginners, intermediate members, and competitive fighters. These high-energy sessions help members improve physically while learning the technical side of boxing in a motivating environment.

  • Boxing fundamentals
  • Mitt work & heavy bag training
  • Sparring development
  • Footwork & defense
  • Cardio & endurance training
  • Technical boxing instruction

Strength & Conditioning Program

Build Strength. Power. Endurance.

Our Strength & Conditioning Program focuses on athletic performance, explosive power, speed, endurance, agility, and total-body conditioning. Training methods are inspired by elite-level boxing and sports performance programs used by professional athletes and fighters.

  • Functional strength training
  • Speed & agility drills
  • HIIT conditioning
  • Core & endurance development
  • Athletic performance coaching
  • Weight-loss & fitness training

Youth Development Program

Building the Next Generation
