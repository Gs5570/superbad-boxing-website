Our Story

A Gym Built on Detroit's Fighting Spirit

SuperBad Boxing Gym is more than a place to train — it is a community built on discipline, hard work, and the fighting spirit of Detroit. Founded by individuals with deep roots in the boxing world, our gym was created to help athletes, youth, and everyday people become stronger mentally, physically, and emotionally.

We believe boxing changes lives. Through structure, mentorship, and championship-level training, we help our members build confidence, resilience, and purpose both inside and outside the ring.

Our Mission

"To empower individuals through boxing, fitness, mentorship, and leadership while building champions in life before champions in the ring."

Detroit Born

Community at the Core

Whether your goal is to compete, get in shape, learn self-discipline, or become part of a positive community — SuperBad Boxing Gym provides the environment and support needed to grow.

We welcome all ages, all levels, and all backgrounds. This gym was built for Detroit, and Detroit built us.
