OUR SERVICES

We offer a wide range of programs to help our members succeed. All ages, levels, and backgrounds are welcome to join.

BOXING

Train like a fighter in an authentic Detroit boxing environment built on discipline, hard work, and championship mentality. Our Boxing Training program is designed for all skill levels — from beginners learning the fundamentals to amateur and professional fighters preparing for competition. At SuperBad Boxing Gym, members develop real boxing skills including footwork, defense, combinations, ring IQ, conditioning, and mental toughness through structured, high-energy training sessions led by experienced coaches and fighters. Whether your goal is to compete, get in shape, build confidence, or challenge yourself mentally and physically, our boxing program pushes you to become stronger inside and outside the ring.

STRENGTH AND CONDITIONING

Our Strength & Conditioning program is built to develop explosive power, speed, endurance, and total-body athleticism. Inspired by elite-level boxing and sports performance training, these sessions are designed to push your physical and mental limits while helping you become stronger, faster, and more resilient. Whether you’re an athlete preparing for competition or someone looking to improve overall fitness, lose weight, or build confidence, our coaches create an intense and motivating environment focused on real results. Every workout combines functional strength training, boxing conditioning, agility drills, and high-intensity exercises to maximize performance and help you reach your goals.

PRIVATE LESSONS

Take your training to the next level with personalized coaching tailored specifically to your goals. Our Private Lessons program provides focused, one-on-one instruction designed to accelerate your progress and help you train smarter, harder, and more effectively. Whether you are a beginner learning the basics, an athlete preparing for competition, or someone looking to improve fitness and confidence, our experienced coaches create customized training sessions built around your individual needs and performance level. Train directly with knowledgeable boxing coaches in a championship-driven environment that emphasizes technique, discipline, conditioning, and personal growth.

Mission Statement

Who we are?

At SuperBad Boxing Gym, we are more than a boxing gym — we are a Detroit-born family and community dedicated to building champions inside and outside the ring. Founded by professional fighters with deep roots in the city, we use boxing as a tool to develop discipline, confidence, leadership, and resilience in people of all ages. Inspired by Detroit’s fighting spirit and legendary boxing culture, we strive to create an environment where hard work, respect, and accountability shape the next generation.

Why People Choose Us?

People choose SuperBad Boxing Gym because we combine elite-level boxing knowledge with a genuine family atmosphere. Whether someone is training to compete, get in shape, build confidence, or simply find direction, our gym provides a supportive environment where everyone is pushed to grow. Our members train alongside champions, experienced coaches, and motivated athletes who inspire one another daily. We are known for our authentic boxing culture, strong mentorship, and commitment to helping every individual reach their full potential

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the youth and community of Detroit through boxing, fitness, mentorship, and opportunity. We aim to build champions in life before champions in the ring by teaching discipline, mental toughness, self-respect, and perseverance. Through structured training, guidance, and positive leadership, we seek to create a safe and motivating environment where individuals can transform their lives and pursue greatness both athletically and personally

Our Team

Our team is led by experienced fighters, trainers, and mentors who understand the dedication required to succeed at the highest level. Co-founded by Tony Harrison and L.J. Harrison, our coaches bring real-world experience, passion, and mentorship to every athlete who walks through our doors. We believe in leading by example and creating strong relationships with our members through encouragement, accountability, and hard work.

  • Strength and conditioning
