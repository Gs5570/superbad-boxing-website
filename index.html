OUR SERVICES

We offer a wide range of programs to help our members succeed. All ages, levels, and backgrounds are welcome to join.

BOXING

We offer a range of boxing services to suit every fitness journey. Our signature boxing class, Round By Round, combines cardio, strength training, and technique, welcoming all skill levels to build confidence while burning calories! For more personalized attention, our semi-private coaching provides focused instruction in a small group setting, blending individual support with the energy of a team. If you’re looking for fully customized training, our one-on-one boxing coaching sessions are tailored to help you master technique, enhance fitness, and reach your specific goals with expert guidance..

STRENGTH AND CONDITIONING

Our strength and conditioning training services are designed to help you build strength, power, and confidence. Inspired by boxing sports science our strength training program, combines expert coaching with a high-energy atmosphere, making it perfect for all levels. Our one-on-one coaching offers customized guidance to help you refine your technique, hit personal records, and achieve your unique fitness goals..

PRIVATE LESSONS

Our private coaching sessions provide focused instruction for a limit of four people in a small group setting, ensuring you get the support you need while enjoying the camaraderie of others. Each person receives tailored training and a full-body workout suitable for all fitness levels. For those that don’t want all the attention on themselves, semi -private training is a great alternative. Combining strength training and boxing, each class blends perfectly, leaving you feeling unstoppable.

Mission Statement

Who we are?

At SuperBad Boxing Gym, we are more than a boxing gym — we are a Detroit-born family and community dedicated to building champions inside and outside the ring. Founded by professional fighters with deep roots in the city, we use boxing as a tool to develop discipline, confidence, leadership, and resilience in people of all ages. Inspired by Detroit’s fighting spirit and legendary boxing culture, we strive to create an environment where hard work, respect, and accountability shape the next generation.

Why People Choose Us?

People choose SuperBad Boxing Gym because we combine elite-level boxing knowledge with a genuine family atmosphere. Whether someone is training to compete, get in shape, build confidence, or simply find direction, our gym provides a supportive environment where everyone is pushed to grow. Our members train alongside champions, experienced coaches, and motivated athletes who inspire one another daily. We are known for our authentic boxing culture, strong mentorship, and commitment to helping every individual reach their full potential

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the youth and community of Detroit through boxing, fitness, mentorship, and opportunity. We aim to build champions in life before champions in the ring by teaching discipline, mental toughness, self-respect, and perseverance. Through structured training, guidance, and positive leadership, we seek to create a safe and motivating environment where individuals can transform their lives and pursue greatness both athletically and personally

Our Team

Our team is led by experienced fighters, trainers, and mentors who understand the dedication required to succeed at the highest level. Co-founded by Tony Harrison and L.J. Harrison, our coaches bring real-world experience, passion, and mentorship to every athlete who walks through our doors. We believe in leading by example and creating strong relationships with our members through encouragement, accountability, and hard work.

What We Offer:

At SuperBad Boxing Gym

  • Elite boxing training for beginners, amateurs, and professional fighters
  • Youth development and mentorship programs
  • Strength and conditioning
  • Sparring and competition preparation
  • Fitness and weight-loss boxing programs
  • Personal training and athlete developmen
  • A motivating community focused on growth, discipline, and excellence

Our goal is to provide every member — regardless of age or experience — with the tools, coaching, and environment needed to become stronger mentally, physically, and emotionally.
